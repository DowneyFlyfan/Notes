# Space
*   Hilbert Space

1.  Function Inner Product in Hilbert Space\\[<f, g> = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) g^\*(x) dx]  
    
2.  Definition  
    如果 (\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |f(x)|^2 dx < \infty$, 那么 (f(x) \in L^2(\mathbb R)$ **(Complete, 完备性)**  
      
    其中(L^2(\mathbb R)$就是**Hilbert Space
